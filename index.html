About AgriTrace+

Modernizing Philippine Livestock Management

AgriTrace+ is a comprehensive digital platform developed to revolutionize how the Department of Agriculture monitors, registers, and manages livestock farms across the Philippines.

From small backyard farmers to large commercial operations, our system provides real-time geo-tagged tracking, automated biosecurity alerts, and transparent reporting.

🗺️

Geo-Tagged Mapping

Precise GPS coordinates for every registered farm with real-time density visualization

🚨

Outbreak Alerts

Instant disease outbreak notifications with automated DA Officer dispatch

📊

Data Analytics

Comprehensive reports and analytics for informed policy decisions

💊

IoT Monitoring

Arduino sensor integration for real-time livestock health data

🌾

Built for Filipino Farmers

Serving farmers and DA officers across all regions of the Philippines with a modern, easy-to-use system.

Core Features

Everything You Need to Manage Livestock

📋

Farm Registration

Streamlined digital registration with GPS tagging, animal census, and document verification for all types of livestock farms.

🗺️

Live Geo Map

Interactive real-time map showing all registered farms with color-coded animal density overlays and incident alerts.

🚨

Incident Reporting

Immediate disease outbreak reporting with priority escalation, automatic DA Officer notification, and resolution tracking.

💊

Health Monitoring

Arduino IoT sensor integration tracking temperature, humidity, ammonia, CO₂, feed and water levels in real time.

📈

Reports & Analytics

Comprehensive exportable reports covering farm registrations, livestock counts, incident history, and DA inspections.

📢

Public Reporting

Anonymous public reporting portal for biosecurity threats near farms — community-powered outbreak detection.
